ü§ñ Machine Learning Methodology

Multiple Algorithm Approaches

Our system employs four complementary machine learning algorithms to maximize prediction accuracy:

Logistic Regression Probabilistic linear classifier for binary classification
Random Forest Ensemble of decision trees for robust predictions
XGBoost Gradient boosting framework for high accuracy
Support Vector Machine Kernel-based classifier for non-linear patterns

Data Preprocessing

  • Feature Scaling: StandardScaler normalization for consistent model performance
  • Train-Test Split: 80-20 split for reliable performance evaluation
  • Stratified Sampling: Maintains class distribution in train/test sets
  • Cross-Validation: 5-fold stratified k-fold validation to prevent overfitting

Hyperparameter Tuning

Each algorithm undergoes GridSearchCV optimization with comprehensive parameter grids to identify the best hyperparameter combinations. The final model is selected based on F1-score, which balances precision and recall for medical predictions.

Performance Metrics

All models are evaluated using multiple metrics:

  • Accuracy: Overall correctness of predictions
  • Precision: True positive rate among positive predictions
  • Recall: True positive rate among actual positives
  • F1-Score: Harmonic mean of precision and recall
  • ROC-AUC: Area under the receiver operating characteristic curve

üìä Patient Health Parameters

The system analyzes 11 key health indicators to make risk predictions:

Age

Patient's age in years. Age is a significant risk factor for CAD.

Sex

Biological sex (0=Female, 1=Male). Gender differences affect CAD risk profiles.

Anaemia

Presence of anaemia (0=No, 1=Yes). Anaemia affects cardiac workload.

Creatinine Phosphokinase (CPK)

Enzyme level (mcg/L). Elevated CPK indicates muscle or cardiac damage.

Diabetes

Diabetic status (0=No, 1=Yes). Diabetes significantly increases CAD risk.

Ejection Fraction

Percentage (0-100%). Measures percentage of blood pumped by the heart per beat.

High Blood Pressure

Hypertension status (0=No, 1=Yes). Major risk factor for cardiovascular disease.

Platelets

Count (kiloplatelets/mL). Platelets play a role in blood clotting and CAD.

Serum Creatinine

Level (mg/dL). Indicates kidney function, which affects cardiac health.

Serum Sodium

Level (mEq/L). Electrolyte balance affects heart function.

Smoking

Smoking status (0=Never, 1=Current/Former). Major risk factor for CAD.
